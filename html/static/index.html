BC

Welcome to BC, please follow the steps below to get setup;

  1. Get BC for your device;

    • Android - Google Play Store or APK
    • iOS - coming soon!
    • JVM - JAR
    • Linux - ARM (32, 64) or Intel (32, 64)
    • Mac - ARM (32, 64) or Intel (32, 64)
    • Windows - ARM (32, 64) or Intel (32, 64)

  2. Generate your key pair consisting of a public key (which will be registered with your account) and a private key (which does not leave your possession).

    $> ./bc alias
    Creating keystore under /Users/username/bc
    Enter keystore password:
    Confirm keystore password:
    Generating RSA 4096 Public/Private Key Pair
    Successfully created keystore!

    -----BEGIN RSA PUBLIC KEY-----
    ...
    ...
    -----END RSA PUBLIC KEY-----

    Register your public key and a chosen alias at https://bc.aletheiaware.com/alias
